    Who Is Pooja Hegde

    Biographie

    In India, Pooja Hegde is one of the leading actors and models. Born On 13th October 1990. Pooja Hegde was crowned as the second runner-up at the Miss Universe India 2010 contest. She was born and raised in Mumbai. Her parents are Manjunath and Latha Hegde.

    she is a Mumbai native. Originating from Mangalore in Karnataka, they currently live in Mumbai. In addition, she has an older brother named Rishabh Hegde. Her specialty is dance and fashion, and she is a frequent participant in intercollegiate competitions.
