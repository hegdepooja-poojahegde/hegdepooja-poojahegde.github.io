Pooja Hegde

Pooja Hegde is an Indian model and flim actress. She make her acting debut in Tamil film Mugamoodi (2012). She was born in Mumbai, Maaharashtra. Her Family Manjunath Hegde, Latha Hegde and Rishabh Hegde Her other tougue is Tulu. she went to M. M. K. College. She regularly participating in intercollegiate dance competitions and fashion shows. A former beauty pageant contestant.

She competed in the Miss India 2009 competition, but was eliminated in an early round. She re-applied the following year and was the second runner-up in the Miss Universe India 2010 competition, while also being crowned Miss India South Glamorous Hair 2010 in the subsidiary competition.

Career

She made her acting debut in Mysskin's Tamil superhero film Mugamoodi (2012) opposite Jiiva, female lead character of Shakthi. Her Family helped practice Tamil dialogue for the film by writing and memorizing words in English, adding that the similarity between Tamil and her native tongue Tulu was also useful. She earned a nomination for the Best Tamil Female Debut Actress at the 2nd South Indian International Movie Awards.

Her second film, Oka Laila Kosam (2014), the Telugu film, featured her opposite Naga Chaitanya. she earned the nomination for Best Actress at the 62nd Filmfare Awards (South).

In 2014, Mukunda, opposite Varun Tej. In 2016, Mohenjo Daro [Bollywood Debut Movie], opposite Hrithik Roshan. In 2017, Duvvada Jagannadham (DJ), opposite Allu Arjun. In 2018, Rangasthalam, Special appearance in the Jigelu Rani song. Saakshyam, opposite Bellamkonda Sreenivas. Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava, opposite Jr. NTR. In 2019, Maharshi, opposite Mahesh Babu. Gaddalakonda Ganesh, featuring her as Sridevi. Housefull 4, featuring her as Rajkumari Mala and Pooja. In 2020, Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo, opposite Allu Arjun.
